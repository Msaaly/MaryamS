About Me

I am a data scientist with a solid background in engineering, currently pursuing my Master of Science in Analytics at the Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech). My engineering background provides me with a strong analytical foundation, which I combine with my passion for creativity and technology to solve complex problems. I have a keen interest in exploring how data science can be used to drive innovation across various domains, including art, business, and technology.

Currently residing in Calgary, I actively engage with the vibrant data science community, seeking out opportunities to blend data-driven insights with artistic expression. Whether it's developing machine learning models, creating data visualizations, or experimenting with generative art, I aim to use data not only to tell compelling stories but also to unlock new creative possibilities. With a blend of technical expertise and artistic curiosity, I continue to push the boundaries of how data science and creativity can intersect to create meaningful, impactful work.
